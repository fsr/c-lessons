Short description

The course extends the programming skills in terms of both language features and developing techniques. The participants will develop a small and fancy dungeon game.

Intended audience

The course adresses former participants of the cintroduction course and higher semester students who want to refresh or extent their knowledge of the C programming language.

Knowledge requirements

Students should be able to write correct and working C programs containing programming strategies taught in the cindroduction course.

Time requirements

The course will take place for 6 weeks. There are two lessons (4 SWS) per week.
